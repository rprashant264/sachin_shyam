About

Mr. Sachin Shyam, the accomplished director of Shyam Polycom, stands as a formidable figure in the business landscape of Shamli. Known for his exceptional business acumen and strategic leadership, Sachin has steered Shyam Polycom towards remarkable success. His journey in entrepreneurship has been greatly influenced by the invaluable teachings of his father, Hari Shyam Jangid, an eminent businessman and a mentor to Sachin. Hari Shyam Jangid's profound insights and guidance have played a pivotal role in shaping Sachin's approach to business. Together, their dynamic partnership has not only contributed to the growth of Shyam Polycom but has also left an indelible mark on the business community in Shamli, establishing the Shyam family as a symbol of entrepreneurial excellence and success.
